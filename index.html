Step 1

Look at the example

Example sources: Open first responder PDF Open EMT BLS source

Rule: if you learned the information from a source, cite it. This is true even when you put the information into your own words.

!

Click the highlighted citations in the example. Each one will show you how the writer used a quote, paraphrase, or citation from the original source.

Being an EMT or paramedic means dealing with emergencies and helping people right away. The Bureau of Labor Statistics says EMTs and paramedics and sometimes also transport patients to medical facilities . The BLS also explains that the job can be stressful and physically hard, and that workers may have to work nights, weekends, and holidays . The CareerOneStop source also shows that people entering this field need to pay attention to training, licensing, or certification requirements . Together, these sources show that becoming an EMT is not just about wanting to help people. It also requires training, careful preparation, and an understanding of the working conditions.
